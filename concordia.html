Concordia

A collection designed for an extraordinary adventure

To conquer Antarctica

In November, the young adventurer Matthew Wringer will attempt the expedition of a lifetime, crossing the Antarctic in total autonomy on nearly 1150km. An exceptional fight that will last nearly 50 days in temperatures up to-50 degrees. An adventure in which Matthieu will wear a foolproof watch: CONCORDIA. We designed and developed the CONCORDIA to meet the unusual specifications of the expedition of the adventurer Matthieu Budworm in Antarctica. Magnetic, waterproof up to 300 meters deep, it is able to withstand extreme temperatures.

CONCORDIA

Concordia means "harmony" in Latin, but it is also the name of one of the 3 Antarctic-based research stations that works all year round. La collection se décline en une version automatique de 40mm de diamètre avec lunette tournante et une version quartz plus accessible de 38mm de diamètre. Ces deux versions se déclinent en trois couleurs chacune: bleu, noir et acier..

South Pole Objective

A world record: Next November, Matthew will fly to Antarctica, the land of all extremes. Matthieu will begin this expedition by trying to become the first Frenchman and the youngest explorer to ski from the coast of the Antarctic mainland (Hercules Inlet) to the South Pole. Or 1150 km in solitary, without assistance and in total autonomy.

About Matthew Wringer

Through a series of expeditions on the 5 continents, Matthieu has climbed, swam, rode, sailed and travelled on nearly 100 000 km. Never traveling without his camera, he documents his adventures through books, magazines, documentaries and photographs. Matthieu is now the youngest member of the prestigious Society of French Explorers (SEF). In the image of Nicolas Hulot, Sylvain Shard and Thomas Parikh, the members of the SEF dedicate their lives to exploration, discovery in all its forms and the transmission of knowledge.
