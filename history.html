Simple

LAUNCHED IN 2014 AND BASED IN PARIS, CHARLIE IS A MODERN AND INDEPENDENT WATCHMAKING HOUSE WHOSE AMBITION IS SIMPLE: TO OFFER PRODUCTS THAT ARE BOTH CLASSIC AND CONTEMPORARY, QUALITY AND AT A FAIR PRICE.

A passion for watchmaking

We are Ambroise and Adrien, two childhood friends, passionate about watchmaking and beautiful products. And we put all our enthusiasm and skills to create a unique brand, close to our style and values. What drives us is the creation of timeless pieces with singular lines, made in France with care and meaning. We want to remain independent and offer our collections at the fairest prices, which is why we develop mainly on the Internet.

Reenchant French manufacturing

By its history, France is the real cradle of European watchmaking. We therefore wish to nurture and develop this wonderful heritage by combining both the times of tradition and modernity. Through our values and our products, we want to become the ambassador of a certain French art of living, both chic and cool, which qualifies the watch not only as a measuring instrument, but also as a means of affirming its personality.

Our values

Freedom: We are proud to be a 100% independent company free to be able to act according to our will. Embodied by our flying Stork, freedom is a strong value that rhymes with spontaneity and creativity.

Benevolence Kindness, honesty and respect allow us to establish relationships of trust in duration with our clients, our partners, our collaborators and the whole ecosystem that surrounds us.

Quality: The love of work well done, the attention to detail, the search for the best know-how are all elements that illustrate that quality is a strict priority for Charlie.

Sobriety: The sincerity and authenticity of our approach are illustrated by the sobriety of our creations, where every element of its importance and where superfluity has no place. Less is more.

Dynamism: Our optimism and the energy we put into achieving our vision make Charlie a home whose dynamism and spirit of initiative are strong values.
